Butterballs

Home | Jump to Recipe

Description

Meatball-sized balls of bread crumbs, eggs, cream, and - you guessed it - butter. Typically boiled and served in noodle soup with chicken broth (and chicken, if you're lucky).

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cups sweet cream
  • 2 cups fine bread crumbs, toasted
  • 4 cups white bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp allspice
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs, beaten

Recipe

  1. Pour cream into saucepan and heat to boiling.
  2. Remove from heat and add toasted bread crumbs. Allow to set for 10 minutes.
  3. Combine white bread crumbs, salt, and allspice in a bowl.
  4. Add melted butter, eggs, and cream mixture. Mix well by hand.
  5. Form into walnut sized balls. Cook in noodle soup or use in noodle & chicken casserole.
