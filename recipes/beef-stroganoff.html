Beef Stroganoff

Home | Jump to Recipe

Description

Creamy and savory sauce with beef and mushrooms served on top of egg noodles. A tasty and simple rendition of a classic dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz boneless beef sirloin steak
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium white onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 8 oz sour cream
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cubes dry beef bouillon, crushed
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 12-16 oz egg noodles, cooked

Recipe

  1. Slice beef across the grain into bite-size chunks. Coat lightly with flour, salt, & pepper (use more flour as needed).
  2. Heat oil in large saucepan over med-high heat. Add onion, garlic, and beef; cook and stir about 5 mins or until meat is almost cooked (some pink). While beef cooks, add sour cream, water, and bouillon to a bowl and stir until combined; set aside.
  3. Remove beef from pan; melt butter in pan, then add mushrooms and saute until mushrooms start to get tender, about 5-7 mins.
  4. Add meat mixture back into pan and cook until meat is fully browned or reaches desired doneness (approx. 1-3 mins). Reduce heat to med.
  5. Stir sour cream mixture into meat mixture and bring to simmer. Reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 15-30 mins until sauce thickens and meat reaches desired tenderness. Salt & pepper to taste.
  6. Serve over noodles.
