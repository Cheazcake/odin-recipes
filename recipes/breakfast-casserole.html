Breakfast Casserole

Home | Jump to Recipe

Description

My grandma's breakfast casserole. Perfect for brunch with the family or meal prep for those who don't plan on sharing this delectable delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-oz) pkg. Potatoes O'Brien
  • 16 eggs
  • Salt, as desired
  • Pepper, as desired
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups ham, cubed or 1 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup Velveeta cheese, cut into small cubes
  • 2 cups Cheddar or Swiss cheese, shredded
  • Paprika (optional)

Recipe

The night before:

  1. Thaw potatoes. Place in a greased 9 x 13 inch casserole dish.
  2. Mix eggs with salt and pepper and pour over potatoes
  3. Add onion, green pepper, ham (or bacon), and Velveeta cheese to top of egg mixture.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top of casserole, then sprinkle paprika over the top, if desired.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate.

In the morning:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Remove foil and bake for 30 minutes or longer.
