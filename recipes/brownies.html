Brownies

Home | Jump to Recipe

Description

Sugary, buttery, chocolatey brownies. Irresistible!

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter or margarine
  • 4 tbsp cocoa
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Nuts, chopped (optional)

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Melt butter or margarine in a saucepan and stir in cocoa.
  3. Add sugar, flour, eggs, vanilla, salt, and nuts. Mix well.
  4. Pour into greased 9 x 13 inch pan.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes.
