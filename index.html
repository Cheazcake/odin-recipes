About the Recipes

Most recipes are from "Cooking with the Germans from Russia", a cookbook assembled by my extended family, the descendants of ethnic Germans who left their homes along the Volga River in Russia and settled in western Nebraska in the early 1900's. Dozens of people contributed, including my parents, aunts, uncles, grandparents, and even great-grandparents, who were the first generation to be raised in the United States.

Not all of the recipes are Volga German dishes; these are just some recipes from the American descendants of one Volga German family. Recipes that are "traditional" Volga German fare are indicated as such on this page.
